Why learn Linux?

Linux is everywhere. From servers hosting websites and cloud computing platforms to embedded systems in Internet of Things (IoT) devices, mastering Linux is an invaluable skill that can open doors to various career opportunities. Learning Linux can help you gain a deeper understanding of how computers work, improve your problem-solving skills, and empower you to harness the full potential of your devices.

What will you learn?

  1. Basic Linux commands: You'll learn fundamental command-line tools to navigate the file system, create and manipulate files and more. These commands are essential for interacting with a Linux system efficiently.
  2. Text filters: Discover the power of text manipulation in Linux. We'll explore tools like grep, cut, and tr that allow you to process and transform text with precision
  3. Permissions: Understanding how permissions work is crucial for maintaining security and privacy on a Linux system. You'll delve into the world of permissions, ownership, and access control.
  4. Linux Distributions: Get to know the various Linux distributions available and their unique characteristics. We'll introduce you to a few popular distros.
