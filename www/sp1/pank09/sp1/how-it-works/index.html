How International Volunteer HQ works

Ready to see the world and make a difference? Leave it to us, we’ve done it 132,096 times!

With International Volunteer HQ, you get to live an unforgettable adventure while helping others — and you don’t have to worry about a thing apart from showing up!

Step 1. Choose a destination and a volunteer project

What country would you like to explore? What are the causes you care about? What’s your budget? How long do you want to volunteer for? Ask yourself all these questions and choose the program that best suits you. Browse by either searching by your preferred destination, type of project, or if you’re undecided you can get personalized recommendations from our Volunteer Travel Experts.

View all programmes

Step 2. Apply online for free

It only takes 5 minutes to apply for your preferred volunteer program! By submitting a free application online, our team can check that you’re eligible for your chosen program and that there is availability for your chosen dates. You can expect to hear back from us within 2 working days.

You can always make free changes to your destination, volunteer project, start date or duration later on if you change your mind.

Who is eligible to volunteer? For most programs, previous volunteer experience is not required as you’ll receive training. However, some programs may have language, age or qualification requirements.

Apply now

Step 3. Start getting prepared

You will have your very own Volunteer Travel Expert guiding you every step of the way. They will help get you ready for your volunteer trip by answering your questions and ensuring you complete our pre-departure online training and preparation checklist. This online training will help you to understand how to volunteer responsibly, stay safe, reduce culture shock, set expectations, and much more.

Our comprehensive Program Guide covers everything you need to know about your destination before you depart, from how much spending money you’ll need and where to buy a SIM card, to what you should pack.

You can also book a whole host of awesome excursions, tours, language lessons, accommodation upgrades (where available) and other fun activities to fill your spare time at discounted prices via your MyIVHQ account!

Our comprehensive step-by-step preparation checklist will guide you through the process and we’ll send you timely email reminders so you don’t miss anything important.

Step 4. Embark on your adventure and make a difference abroad

You’re all set to go! You’ve completed your training, booked your activities and tours, been introduced to our local team (who will pick you up from the airport or from another pre-arranged meeting point) and you’re all packed!

Once you land at your destination, IVHQ’s local team is your main point of contact, but your IVHQ Program Manager will still be here to support you via phone or email if needed.

A piece of advice from past volunteers: “Go with an open mind and a humble heart. Be ready for an incredibly eye-opening experience”.

Step 5. Return home and start thinking where to volunteer next!

If there’s one thing that the IVHQ community agrees on, it’s that volunteering is a life changing experience. After finishing your first volunteering trip, you’ll just want to do it again!
