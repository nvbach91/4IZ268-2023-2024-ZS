  • Burkina Faso

  • Gambia
  • Guinea
  • Guinea-Bissau
  • Liberia
  • Mali
  • Niger
  • Nigeria
  • Senegal
  • Sierra Leone
  • South Africa (From November 1, 2023)
  • Togo

60 days

  • Barbados
  • Botswana
  • Eswatini
  • Guyana
  • Jamaica
  • Kenya
  • Lesotho
  • Malawi
  • Mauritius
  • Namibia

  • Singapore
  • Tanzania
  • Trinidad and Tobago
  • Uganda
  • Zimbabwe

Documents and Forms

A passport with 6 months validity is required after the traveler enters Ghana.

Public Health Requirements

  • Mandatory proof of Yellow Fever vaccination
  • Proof of pre-departure COVID-19 test is not required
