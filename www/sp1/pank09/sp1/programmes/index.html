Volunteer opportunities in Ghana

Discover International Volunteer HQ’s incredible Ghana volunteer abroad program. Choose from a wide range of trusted Ghana volunteer projects, including Teaching, Childcare, Medical and Construction. Volunteers are based in and around the Accra region, on the Atlantic coast of West Africa. With affordable Program Fees and top rated volunteer projects trusted by over 132,000 travelers, IVHQ is the best volunteer organization in Ghana.

Agriculture in Ghana

  • Duration: 1 - 24 weeks
  • Starts: on the 1st and 15th of every month
  • Based: in rural Ghana
  • For: volunteers aged 18+ and families

Help farmers become more sustainable in all aspects of their operations, with the resulting food and produce going towards local childcare placements.

Childcare Volunteer Program in Ghana

  • Duration: 1 - 24 weeks
  • Starts: on the 1st and 15th of every month
  • Based: in rural Ghana
  • For: volunteers aged 18+ and families

Volunteer with young school children in rural parts of Ghana before and after school, assisting with meals, providing care, assisting with homework, and informal teaching English through play therapy and games.

Apply

Healthcare Outreach and Education in Ghana

  • Duration: 1 - 24 weeks
  • Starts: on the 15th of every month
  • Based: in rural Ghananian communities
  • For: volunteers aged 18+ and families

A medical project with a difference. This is a one week roving medical campaign, visiting rural communities and helping to provide malaria treatment and prevention, as well education around nutrition, sexual health and hygiene.
