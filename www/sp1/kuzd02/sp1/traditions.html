Explore Icelandic Traditions and Culture

Local Celebrations

Learn about traditional Icelandic celebrations, such as Þorrablót, a midwinter festival where locals gather to enjoy traditional foods like hákarl (fermented shark) and sing traditional songs.

Culinary Specialties

Discover the unique culinary specialties of Iceland, including dishes like rúgbrauð (rye bread), hangikjöt (smoked lamb), and kleinur (deep-fried pastry).

Arts and Crafts

Explore the rich artistic traditions of Iceland, from traditional Icelandic sagas to contemporary visual arts. Learn about the influence of nature and folklore on Icelandic artistic expression.
