Useful Tips for Traveling to Iceland

Tip 1: Suitable Clothing

It depends on the season, but even in summer, it's good to have warm clothing as the weather can be variable. Also, bring a waterproof jacket and comfortable shoes for walks.

Tip 2: Safety Guidelines

Remember safety guidelines when exploring nature. Avoid lingering in hazardous areas and respect local warnings.
