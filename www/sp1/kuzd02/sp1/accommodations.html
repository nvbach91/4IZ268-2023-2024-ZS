Explore Different Accommodation Options

Hotels

Discover luxurious hotels with stunning views. Amenities include spacious rooms, on-site dining, and wellness facilities. Prices vary based on room type and season. Locations range from city centers to scenic countryside.

  • Hotel Aurora: A 5-star hotel in Reykjavik with panoramic views of the Northern Lights. Prices start at $300 per night.
  • Mountain View Resort: Nestled in the highlands, offering a peaceful retreat. Prices from $250 per night.

Guesthouses

Experience cozy guesthouses with a personal touch. Enjoy comfortable rooms, homemade breakfast, and friendly hosts. Prices are generally more affordable than hotels. Guesthouses can be found in both urban and rural areas.

  • Sunset Guesthouse: Located near the Golden Circle, offering a warm atmosphere. Prices from $120 per night, including breakfast.
  • Harbor Haven: A seaside guesthouse in Akureyri, known for its hospitality. Prices start at $100 per night.

Apartments

Choose apartments for a more independent stay. These accommodations provide fully-equipped kitchens, living spaces, and various sizes to suit different group sizes. Prices are often based on the duration of your stay.

  • Cosy Downtown Loft: A modern loft in Reykjavik's city center. Prices from $150 per night.
  • Golden Valley Chalet: A spacious chalet near Geysir, perfect for families. Weekly rates starting at $800.

Campsites

For those seeking adventure, campsites offer a unique experience. Bring your camping gear and enjoy Iceland's nature. Prices are usually per person, and facilities vary from basic to more developed sites.

  • Wilderness Camp: Basic campsite in Thingvellir National Park. $15 per person per night.
  • Adventure Base Camp: Well-equipped campsite near Vik. $20 per person per night.
