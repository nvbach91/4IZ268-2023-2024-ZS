Locations

  • White Residence

    Adress: 308 Negra Arroyo Lane, Albuquerque, New Mexico 87104

    Type: Residence

    Description: The White Residence, located at 308 Negra Arroyo Lane, was the home of the White family including Walter, his wife Skyler, their son Walt Jr. and their infant daughter, Holly.

  • Jesse's House

    Adress: 9809 Margo Street, Albuquerque, New Mexico 87104

    Type: Residence

    Description: The house, located at 9809 Margo Street, was originally owned by Jesse's aunt Ginny. Jesse moved in while his aunt was dying of lung cancer. After Jesse's aunt passed away, his parents took ownership of the house and allowed Jesse to stay there. The phone number was [143 - 3369|(505) 148 - 3369].

  • Superlab

    Adress: 1613 Candelaria Rd NE, Albuquerque, New Mexico 87107

    Type: Laboratory

    Description: The Superlab is a large-scale laboratory facility that was located underneath the Lavandería Brillante industrial laundry in Albuquerque, New Mexico. The lab was proposed by Lydia Rodarte-Quayle and financed by Gustavo Fring and Peter Schuler, with the project costing over $8 million.

  • Los Pollos Hermanos, Albuquerque

    Adress: 12000 – 12100 Coors Rd SW, Albuquerque, New Mexico 87045

    Type: Fast food restaurant

    Description: Los Pollos Hermanos, Albuquerque is the flagship restaurant of the Los Pollos Hermanos chain. It had many visitors from employees of Gus' other business, namely Walter White, The Cousins, and Mike Ehrmantraut. DEA agent Hank Schrader also staked out the restaurant to monitor Fring.

  • A1A Car Wash

    Adress: 9516 Snow Heights Cir NE, Albuquerque, New Mexico 87112

    Type: Car Wash

    Description: The A1A Car Wash is an Albuquerque car wash owned, for many years, by Bogdan Wolynetz. When he was a high school chemistry teacher, Walter White worked part-time at the car wash as a cashier before deciding to quit after learning of his cancer diagnosis. The car wash was later bought by Walt and Skyler White as a money laundering facility for Walt's illegally procured gains. After they have been helped, customers are told to "have an A1 day" (the motto of the car wash).

  • J.P. Wynne High School

    Adress: 301 Loma Colorado NE, Rio Rancho, New Mexico 87124

    Type: High School

    Description: J. P. Wynne High School is a public high school located in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Walter White was formerly employed as a chemistry teacher at the school. It is also the school where Walter White Jr. and Jesse Pinkman attended as students.
