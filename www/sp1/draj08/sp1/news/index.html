BWT Alpine F1 Race results: GP Las Vegas USA

The BWT Alpine F1 Team delivered a noteworthy performance at the Las Vegas Grand Prix, gaining a total of 12 points in a race marked by both challenges and highlights. Esteban Ocon's display of skill was particularly impressive as he orchestrated a remarkable climb from the sixteenth position on the starting grid to secure a commendable fourth-place finish at the chequered flag.

The Saturday night Grand Prix, set against the stunning backdrop of the city lights, provided an engaging spectacle that unfolded with incidents in the early stages. During the opening lap, Esteban Ocon demonstrated exceptional racecraft, advancing by eight positions to secure the eighth spot, while Pierre Gasly adeptly maintained his starting position of fourth.

BWT Alpine F1 Race results: GP Brazil

The BWT Alpine F1 Team secured a commendable seven points in today's São Paulo Grand Prix, showcasing a resilient performance at Interlagos. Pierre Gasly exhibited remarkable skill, navigating from fifteenth on the starting grid to an impressive seventh-place finish at the checkered flag. Simultaneously, Esteban Ocon executed a strong Sunday effort, progressing from fourteenth on the grid to secure the tenth position.

The race dynamics took a dramatic turn with a multi-car incident on the opening lap, prompting a red flag. Both Esteban and Pierre demonstrated adept reactions, skillfully navigating through the chaos to gain several positions. Upon the restart, Pierre continued his ascent, gaining two more places to secure the ninth position, while Esteban maintained a solid performance, advancing to tenth.

From there, strategy dominated the 71-lap race with Esteban on a three-stop and Pierre on a two-stop strategy.

BWT Alpine F1 Race results: GP Mexico

The BWT Alpine F1 Team secured a valuable point in today's Mexico City Grand Prix, as Esteban Ocon exhibited a resilient performance by recovering from the back of the grid to clinch tenth place at the checkered flag. Meanwhile, Pierre Gasly engaged in a race-long battle for points, ultimately concluding the race in eleventh position.

The race took a significant turn when a red flag was triggered on lap 34 due to Kevin Magnussen's forceful impact, colliding with the trackside wall at Turn 8. During the red flag period, Estaban opted for tyre change going from Hards to Mediums. Managing the newly fitted Mediums until the race's conclusion, Esteban executed a clinical overtaking maneuver on Nico Hülkenberg into Turn 1 five laps from the end, securing the final point.

BWT Alpine F1 Race results: GP COTA USA

The BWT Alpine F1 Team secured four points during the United States Grand Prix today, courtesy of Pierre Gasly's commendable eighth-place finish at the Circuit of the Americas in Austin, Texas. Unfortunately, Esteban Ocon was forced to retire from the race on the seventh lap following substantial damage incurred during an incident on the opening lap, impacting his A523 significantly.

With a strong start off the line, Ocon gained multiple positions up the hill into Turn 1. Regrettably, on the descent toward Turn 2, he became entangled in a racing incident with Oscar Piastri.

Pierre Gasly, despite initially dropping to tenth, by the conclusion of the opening lap, ascended to eighth, capitalizing on the retirements of both Esteban Ocon and Oscar Piastri.
