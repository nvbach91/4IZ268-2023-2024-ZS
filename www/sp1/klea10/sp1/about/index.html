ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Anya and I'm an aspiring web designer. I'm 20 years old. I am a student of the High School of Economics in Prague, Faculty of Computer Science. My passion for technology started long before university classes, when I was first introduced to the world of computers and programming.

From the very beginning, I was fascinated by the idea of creating something new and in demand. Computer science became for me not only a specialty, but also a way of thinking. It gave me the opportunity to delve into the world of algorithms, data structures and cybersecurity - all of which fascinates me and motivates me to develop.

My studies at the Department of Computer Science have been a source of endless inspiration and opportunity. During my studies, I learn about the latest technologies, work on projects, look for solutions to complex problems and learn to apply knowledge in practice.
