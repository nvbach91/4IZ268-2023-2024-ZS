About

I welcome the reader once again to my blog, dedicated to the military history of mankind. Influenced by videogames, documentaries and movies, i've always been fascinated by this aspect of our past.

On this particular website, we turn our attention to the renowned Battle of Austerlitz, a pivotal moment in the Napoleonic Wars that unfolded on December 2, 1805. Described by many historians as "a briliant poker match", it is often considered Napoleons greatest victory. You'll get to read about the prelude to this battle which culminated in the Battle of three Emperors, which saw Napoleon Bonaparte facing off against the Russian and Austrian forces led by Tsar Alexander I and Holy Roman Emperor Francis II.

In addition to the battle itself, we offer you the chance to read up on some of the important figures who took part, or are otherwise asociated with the battle (Napoleon himself included of coures!). For those interested in the kit of the common soldier of the time, we have a page dedicated to describing the gear that soldiers on both sides carried
