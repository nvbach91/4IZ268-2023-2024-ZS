"The Midnight Library" by Matt Haig is a poignant exploration of regret, choices, and the pursuit of happiness. The story revolves around Nora Seed, who finds herself in a mysterious library between life and death, where each book represents a different life she could have lived. Through this magical setting, Haig masterfully delves into themes of self-discovery and the importance of embracing the present. The narrative is emotionally resonant, offering profound insights into the human experience and the power of second chances. Haig's eloquent writing and compelling storytelling make this book a thought-provoking and ultimately uplifting read. I highly recommend this book to pretty much everyone.

"Wicked" is a captivating and imaginative Broadway musical that serves as a prequel to "The Wizard of Oz." Set in the Land of Oz, it tells the tale of the unlikely friendship between Elphaba, the future Wicked Witch of the West, and Glinda, the Good Witch of the North. Their journey navigates themes of friendship, identity, and the perceptions of good and evil. The Apollo Victoria Theatre in London beautifully hosts this magical production, offering a visually stunning experience with its elaborate sets and engaging performances. "Defying Gravity," one of the show's standout numbers, is a powerful and emotionally charged song that captures the essence of the characters' determination and showcases their strength in overcoming societal expectations. Overall, "Wicked" at the Apollo Theatre is a mesmerizing theatrical experience that weaves together spectacle, memorable music, and a compelling narrative.

Living in Prague as a student is an immersive journey through a city that bridges the past and present seamlessly. Every step through its storied streets reveals a captivating blend of history and modernity. From the ornate charm of the Old Town to the vibrant student life, Prague is a mosaic of experiences. The city's architecture, a testament to its rich heritage, serves as both a backdrop to my studies and an inspiration for my personal growth. In Prague, the rhythm of daily life intertwines with the echoes of its past, creating an environment that nurtures both academic endeavors and personal discovery. It's a city that not only educates but also inspires, fostering a unique and enriching student experience.
