What is Trackmania?

Trackmania is a fast-paced, challenging, and rewarding game that is sure to appeal to fans of racing games. The game is easy to pick up and play, but hard to master, and it offers a variety of ways to play. With its active online community and regular updates, Trackmania is a game that will keep you coming back for more. Here are some of the key features of Trackmania: Easy-to-learn, hard-to-master gameplay: The game is easy to pick up and play, but difficult to master. There is a high skill ceiling, so there is always something to strive for. A variety of tracks and cars: The game features a wide variety of tracks and cars, so there is always something new to try. A track editor: The game features a track editor that allows players to create their own tracks. An active online community: The game has an active online community that creates and shares new tracks and challenges on a regular basis.

My Trackmania career

I started competing back in 2013 in Trackmania United Forever for team Dunno. I soon realized that I loved competing and soon I changed to the main competitive game at the time - Trackmania2 Stadium. My first stadium team was Czech team called Speedo. In the summer 2015 I joined my first professional team - team Acer. Unfortunately the company decided to leave esports in the early 2016.¨ After that I played under PlanetKey Dynamics, who I represented at Maniaplanet World Cup 2016 in Paris. I was one of the youngest people to ever been at the world cup. After that I took part in Trackmania ProLeague in 2017. In 2018 I finished 9th at Trackmania World Championships during Paris Games Week. However with the introduction of Trackmania Grand League in 2019 and nonsensical point systems I slowly started losing interest in the game. My last serious tournament was From Zero To Hero in 2020.
