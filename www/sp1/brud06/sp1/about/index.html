My biography

Primary school

I was born in Prague in July 2001 and I've been living in Prague since. My primary school was ZS Angel and I used to play floorball. Number 17 has been with me my entire life. I was born on the 17th, my name day is at 17th and I ended up 17th during my extrance exam for grammar school.

8 years of grammar school

During my 8 years at GEKOM I attended many maths and physics competitions. Maths and physics olympiads, MASO, Naboj and many others. My biggest achievements are successful solver of physics olympiad category C - regional round. And 4th place in International Young Physicist Tournament

University studies

I started my university life right after covid ended. First I went to FIT CTU, but the university was not the right one for me. I started studying at Prague University of Economics and Business, faculty of informatics and statistics the year after. Since then I've learnt a lot. My favorite subjects last academic year were UX Design, Databases and programming in Java. I also managed to earn a scholarship for my results!
