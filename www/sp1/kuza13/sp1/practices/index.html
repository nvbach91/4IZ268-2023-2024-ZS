Key Sustainable Farming Practices

Sustainable farming encompasses various practices that aim to reduce environmental impact and promote healthier agriculture. Some of the key sustainable farming practices include:

  • Organic Farming: Reducing synthetic chemicals for healthier produce.
  • Crop Rotation: Enhancing soil fertility and preventing disease.
  • Precision Agriculture: Using technology for efficient resource management.
