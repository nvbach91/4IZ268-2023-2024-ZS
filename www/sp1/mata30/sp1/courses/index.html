Our Courses

Choose from the variety of cuisines

Embark on a flavorful journey exploring diverse culinary traditions. Discover the secrets of authentic recipes, savor the artful blend of spices, and delight in creating exquisite dishes from around the world.

Chinese cuisine course

Indulge in an enriching journey through the essence of Chinese cuisine. From mastering stir-fries to creating flavorful dumplings, our course immerses you in the art and techniques of authentic Chinese cooking.

Japanese cuisine course

Embark on a culinary voyage into Japan's diverse gastronomic landscape. Dive into the art of sushi crafting, master the artful blend of flavors in ramen, and explore the exquisite balance of umami in traditional Japanese cuisine

Malasysian cuisine course

Embark on an exciting culinary adventure exploring the diverse flavors of Malaysia. From mastering the fiery spice blends of Nasi Lemak to crafting delectable Satay skewers, our course immerses you in the vibrant tapestry of Malaysian cuisine.

South Korea cuisine course

Delve into the artistry of South Korean cuisine with our immersive culinary course. From perfecting the savory flavors of Kimchi to mastering the art of Bibimbap, discover the rich traditions and diverse flavors that define Korea's culinary heritage.

Thai cuisine course

Delve into the aromatic world of Thai cuisine with our immersive culinary course. Learn the art of crafting vibrant dishes like Pad Thai, fragrant Tom Yum soup, and traditional Thai curries, while unraveling the secrets of balancing sweet, spicy, sour, and savory flavors.

Vietnamese cuisine course

Join us on a culinary journey through the heart of Vietnam's gastronomic treasures. Learn the art of crafting savory Pho, perfecting the balance of sweet and savory in Banh Mi, and exploring the intricate flavors of traditional Vietnamese dishes. Immerse yourself in the essence of Vietnamese cuisine and uncover the secrets behind its aromatic and diverse culinary heritage.
