Best players

There are many ways to determine the best players - number of CTWC (classic Tetris world championship) titles, win percentage, high score etc. Therefore, the list below is just my personal selection of four players whom I consider as absolute legends for different reasons.

Jonas Neubauer

Jonas was born in 1981 and unfortunately died in 2021. He is considered to be one of the best Tetris players in the history - he won 7 CTWC championship and he is known as the first player to achieve maxout - score over 999 999. He took huge part in promoting classic NES Tetris and it is fair to say that Tetris is popular (even though it is almost 40 years old) thanks to him and a few other friends.

Due to the fact that the only technique he used was DAS (in his years of playing other techniques weren't a thing yet) and the competition became much harder he won his last title in 2017 and in 2018 CTWC new era came with Joseph Saelee beating him in the final.

Joseph Saelee

Joseph also known as JdMfX_ was born in 2002. He is known as the guy who beat Jonas in the final of 2018 CTWC. He could do that mainly because he was one of the first players to master hypertapping technique and with that he could play after 29 level speed which was considered in previous years as kill screen - unplayable. He won two titles and achieved over 50 NES records in his short career which he ended after loss in final in 2021.

Michael Artiaga

Michael also known as dogplayingtetris was born in 2007. The most notable moment in his career is from 2020 when he won his first CTWC while being just 13 years old - this fact is much more amazing because his opponent was his brother Andy (15 years old at that time). Michael has won it again one year later. He is part of the “Big 6” as well as EricICX - 6 players who absolutely dominated throughout 2022 and 2023. He used hypertapping in the beginning of his career however he learned rolling later on to be able to stand with competition.

Eric Tolt

Eric alias EricICX was born in 2004. He is CTWC Championship title holder from 2022. He unfortunately choked the last one held in 2023 but that does not change the fact that he has currently the highest score of all time in NES Tetris. His other big achievement is being the first player in history to play on glitched colours by completing 1320 lines in one single game. He uses rolling otherwise he would not be able to complete all above.
