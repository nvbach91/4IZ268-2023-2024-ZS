Passion Projects

Project 1: LendMe

"Developed a dynamic and user-centric webpage for item lending, revolutionizing the way we share resources. Through thoughtful design and robust functionality, this platform allows users to seamlessly lend and borrow a diverse array of items, spanning from essential tools for DIY projects to entertaining games for leisure. The intuitive interface simplifies the listing and discovery process, promoting a sense of community and shared responsibility. With a focus on sustainability and collaborative consumption, this webpage encourages users to engage in a new era of neighborly sharing, fostering connections and reducing environmental impact.

Project 2: Mobile todo list

Crafted a mobile to-do list app for efficient task management. Designed with a user-friendly interface, it allows seamless organization of tasks, setting reminders, and enhancing productivity on the go. The app combines simplicity with powerful features, ensuring a smooth and intuitive experience for users looking to stay on top of their daily agenda. Experience the convenience of managing tasks effortlessly – anytime, anywhere.
