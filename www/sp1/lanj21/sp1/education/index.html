CTU FIT

I had the privilege of being part of the Faculty of Information Technology (FIT) at the Czech Technical University (CTU). During my time at FIT, I delved into the exciting world of computer science and technology. The rigorous academic curriculum and hands-on projects provided me with a solid foundation in various aspects of IT. However, I faced challenges, particularly in the realm of mathematics. Despite my dedication and hard work, I encountered difficulties with certain math courses. It was a tough journey, but these challenges became valuable learning experiences, teaching me resilience and the importance of perseverance in the face of obstacles. These moments of struggle not only shaped my academic journey but also contributed to my personal growth. They reinforced my commitment to continuous improvement and the pursuit of knowledge, even in the face of setbacks.

CTU FEL

Another chapter of my educational journey unfolded at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering (FEL) at CTU. FEL became a crucial part of my academic exploration, offering a dynamic environment for delving into the intricate world of electrical engineering and related disciplines. The engaging courses at FEL went beyond traditional lectures, providing hands-on experiences that deepened my understanding of various aspects of electrical engineering. From foundational principles to advanced concepts, the curriculum at FEL was designed to foster critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Moreover, FEL's emphasis on research opportunities allowed me to actively contribute to the ongoing advancements in the field. Collaborative projects became a platform for applying theoretical knowledge to real-world challenges, further expanding my practical expertise. I am proud to share that I successfully completed my bachelor's degree at FEL, marking a significant milestone in my academic journey. In retrospect, my time at FEL was not just an academic endeavor but a transformative experience that shaped my perspective and prepared me for the dynamic landscape of electrical engineering.

VSE

Currently, I am continuing my academic pursuits at the University of Economics, Prague (VSE). This institution has provided me with a broader perspective on business, economics, and management. The diverse learning environment and emphasis on practical applications have been instrumental in shaping my skills and knowledge.
