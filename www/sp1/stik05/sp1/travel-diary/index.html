Travel diary

Week 1: The Northern Highlands

Our journey began amidst the misty mountains of Vietnam's northern highlands. We traversed the winding roads leading to Sapa, a town known for its stunning rice terraces and rich cultural tapestry woven by the local hill tribes. Trekking through this verdant landscape, we were greeted with warm smiles and vibrant traditional attire, creating an unforgettable introduction to Vietnam's natural beauty and cultural diversity.

Further exploration took us through the historic Dien Bien Phu, before reaching the serene beauty of Lai Chau. Each turn on the road revealed panoramic vistas that took our breath away, truly a motorcyclist's dream come true.

Week 2: Toward Central Vietnam

Our second week was marked by the descent from the highlands into Vietnam's central heartland. We ventured through the market towns and rural outposts that dot the landscape, each offering a unique glimpse into the daily rhythms of Vietnamese life. The highlight was the historic Phong Nha-Kẻ Bàng National Park, where we marveled at the otherworldly beauty of its massive caves and underground rivers – nature's artistry on full display.

As we continued southward, the ancient capital of Vinh presented a mosaic of historical sites, from grand temples to solemn war memorials, providing a profound perspective on Vietnam's storied past.

Week 3: The Bustling Coast and Beyond

The final leg of our odyssey brought us to the coastal wonders of Vietnam. The vibrant city of Thanh Hóa offered a mix of urban excitement and serene coastal charm. We soaked in the sun on the sandy stretches of Sam Son Beach and indulged in the freshest seafood.

Our journey concluded in the legendary Ha Long Bay, where we sailed among the limestone karsts rising majestically from emerald waters. As we reflected on the past three weeks, it was clear that Vietnam had etched a permanent place in our hearts with its stunning landscapes and warm-hearted people.
