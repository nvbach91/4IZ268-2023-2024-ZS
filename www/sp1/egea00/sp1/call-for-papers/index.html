Call for papers

Submission portal

The organizing committee is pleased to announce the call for papers for the 3rd GENERIC CONGRESS 2024, 27‑29 February, Prague, Czech Republic.

Authors are invited to submit papers on original, unpublished research in the field. Papers related to this theme are especially encouraged, but we welcome papers related to any of the scientific areas.

Submission rules

  • There is a two-stage submission process. Authors may submit their full papers along with the abstract, but the submission portal will remain open until December 31st.
  • All papers must be submitted only via the online form that is accessible in the Submission portal.
  • Papers must be submitted in Word format.
  • Fill in the submitting author's name and e-mail address properly – these contact details will serve for further correspondence with the author(s).
  • Choose the scientific area with which you want your paper to be associated. If relevant, choose up to two alternative scientific areas.
  • Please choose the desired presentation form (Oral or Poster presentation). Please note that the scientific committee will decide whether your paper is accepted as an oral presentation or as a poster.
  • The full-paper submission may contain up to four A4 pages, with an additional page for references - for both oral or poster presentations. Accepted papers will be published in the Congress proceedings.
  • Authors will be notified about the submission of their abstract by e-mail immediately. If you do not receive confirmation within 24 hours, please check your spam folder. If you receive no confirmation e-mail, please contact organizace@kongresy.eu.
  • Authors will be notified by email whether their paper has been accepted for presentation at the congress.
  • Authors may submit only one paper as the first author, but they may be co-authors of other papers.
