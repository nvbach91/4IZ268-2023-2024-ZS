Welcome message

Dear Attendees,

It is our distinct pleasure to welcome you to the vibrant city of Prague from February 27 to February 29, 2024, for the 3rd Generic Congress. Our gathering in Prague, a city steeped in history and blossoming with culture, promises an enriching tapestry of professional dialogue and networking opportunities.

As we convene within this historic metropolis, your days will be filled with thought-provoking sessions and invaluable insights from leaders across industries. Our comprehensive program is designed to foster innovative discussions, strategic partnerships, and knowledge exchange that will resonate beyond these walls and into the future of our global community.

PRAGUE

Prague is the capital and largest city of the Czech Republic, known for its beautiful architecture, historical ambiance, and cultural significance. It boasts numerous famous cultural attractions like the Prague Castle, the Charles Bridge, Old Town Square with the Prague astronomical clock, the Jewish Quarter, and Petřín hill. The city plays host to a variety of international and local cultural events, conferences, and festivals. Prague's rich history makes it a popular destination for tourists from around the world.

VENUE

The Prague Congress Centre (KCP), originally named the Palace of Culture Prague, is a prominent neofunctionalist building located in the Nusle district of Prague 4, Czech Republic. Built between 1976 and 1981. With a total capacity of 9,300 people across 70 halls, lounges, and meeting rooms, the largest room being the Congress Hall with a capacity of 2,764, the KCP is a major venue for congresses, musicals, meetings, festivals, and concerts.

Getting around

To get around Prague, you can utilize the city's comprehensive and efficient public transportation system which includes the metro, tram, bus, and train networks, along with funiculars and boats. Using these modes of transport, you can efficiently navigate Prague and enjoy its rich history and beautiful landscapes.
