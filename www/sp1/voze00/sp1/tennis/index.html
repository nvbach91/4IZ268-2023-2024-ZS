My tennis journey

How I Started Playing Tennis

I first picked up a tennis racket in the July 2022. It was an instant connection, and since then, I've been playing (almost) non-stop. Tennis helps me fight anxiety and feel stronger, somehow I become very happy after playing tennis for 1 hour.

Tournaments and Competitions

Throughout my rather short journey, I've participated in various tournaments for begginers. Each one was a unique experience, teaching me more about the sport and myself. It was challenging, painful, but the outcome was worth it. I still did not get the 1st place, so I'm coming for it.

My Favorite Tennis Gear

Having the right gear is crucial in tennis. I have my preferences, from rackets to shoes, which have been on my whishlist for a long time.

First one is Yonex Vcore Pro 100L racket, which I've tried recently and fell in love. Very light and easy to play and has perfect control.

Second come Wilson Clay Tennis shoes, which I haven't tried, but heard that they are really comfy. Who does not like a good pair of shoes?

Last but not least - Babolat tennis backpack by Rafael Nadal. Perfect for 3 rackets and other necessities required for training. Love.
