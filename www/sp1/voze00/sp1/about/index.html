Who am I?

My name is Ekaterina, but my friends call me Káťa.

Born in Ufa, Russia, I've moved to Prague when I was 18. Never regretted that. I've loved it here ever since. This city feels like home now, and I've made lots of friends.

This website isn't just about my studies, though. It's also about the things I like to do for fun. I'll share some of my favorite hobbies with you, like playing tennis, gaming, cooking, and crocheting.

I hope you find something here that interests you. Have a look around and enjoy your stay!
