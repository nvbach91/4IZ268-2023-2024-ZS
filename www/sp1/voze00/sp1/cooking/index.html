Recipe 1: Mega Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 300g flour
  • 70 g cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 420 g sugar
  • 125 ml vegetable oil
  • 250 ml buttermilk / kefir (or add 1Tbsp lemon juice into milk and let skim)
  • 180 ml brewed coffee (warm)
  • 350 ml heavy cream 33-35% (bring to a boil)
  • 360 g semi-sweet chocolate (or milk chocolate)

Instructions

  1. First make the ganache. Bring the cream to a boil and pour it over the chocolate. Let melt for 3-5 minutes then stir well until the ganache is smooth. Cool completely. You can refrigerate it for 1 hour or until it thickened.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients. Add eggs, oil, buttermilk (or low-fat kefir), warm coffee, vanilla and beat on medium speed of mixer about 2 minutes.
  3. Grease and line 22-23cm (9 inch) backing pan with parchment paper. Divide the batter evenly and pour into pans. Bake in a preheated oven 180°C 30-35 minuts or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean but don’t over bake the cake! Cool for 15 minutes then remove from the pan and cool completely on wire racks.
  4. Cut and remove the rounded tops of each cake if needed. Spread the ganache.

Recipe 2: Creamy Lemon Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts (if thick cut in half)
  • salt, black pepper to taste
  • garlic powder
  • paprika
  • olive oil
  • a handful (~30 g) of grated Parmesan cheese
  • juice of ½ lemon
  • 200-250 ml half and half or light cream (10-20% fat)
  • 2 tbsp butter (divided)
  • 2 garlic cloves (cut in half)
  • ½ tsp dried basil (or other italian herbs)
  • fresh parsley for garnishing (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the chicken breast, pat dry. Lightly pound it to even out the thickness. Season with salt, pepper, dry garlic, and paprika on both sides. Drizzle with olive oil, rub the spices well into the meat and leave for at least 10 minutes.
  2. Fry the chicken breasts in a well-heated pan with butter (about one tablespoon) over medium-high heat for a couple of minutes on each side until golden brown. It doesn't need to be fully cooked at this point. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius. Transfer the meat to a small baking dish.
  3. In the same pan, heat a small piece of butter, add two sliced cloves of garlic, sauté on low heat for about one minute, then pour in the cream. Bring to a boil, add salt, pepper, basil, grated cheese, and lemon juice to taste. Stir and let it simmer on low heat for 1-2 minutes. Pour the sauce over the chicken and finish cooking in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, depending on the thickness of the chicken breast and your oven.

Recipe 3: Syrniki

Ingredients

  • 250 grams of tvorog (Cottage Cheese)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla sugar
  • paprika
  • 60 grams flour
  • cooking oil

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the 250 grams of tvorog with 1 egg and 1 tbsp of vanilla sugar. Mix these together until they are well incorporated.
  2. Gradually add the 60 grams of flour to the mixture, blending it in until the mixture is uniform. The mixture should be thick enough to form into patties. If it's too wet, add a little more flour.
  3. Wet your hands slightly with water to prevent the mixture from sticking to them. Take a portion of the mixture and form it into a small patty. Roll the patty in the additional 1-2 tablespoons of flour, ensuring it is lightly coated on all sides.
  4. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and add a sufficient amount of cooking oil to lightly coat the bottom of the pan. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the syrniki in the pan. Fry the syrniki for about 5-6 minutes on each side or until they are golden brown. Be gentle when flipping them to avoid breaking.
  5. Serve syrniki hot with sour cream, jam, honey or fresh berries.
