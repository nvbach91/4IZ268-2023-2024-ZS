Naruto Episode 1

Description:

"In the Hidden Leaf Village, a young boy named Naruto Uzumaki is seen causing mischief and being scolded by the villagers due to a mysterious power sealed inside him. Despite this, Naruto dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and earning the title of Hokage, the leader of the village. One day, Naruto graduates from the Ninja Academy and is placed in a team with Sasuke Uchiha, a talented but aloof ninja, and Sakura Haruno, who has a crush on Sasuke. Under the guidance of their mentor, Kakashi Hatake, the team embarks on their first mission to escort a bridge builder named Tazuna to the Land of Waves. During their mission, they encounter Zabuza Momochi, a dangerous rogue ninja, and his apprentice Haku. A fierce battle ensues, revealing the strength and determination of Naruto and his team as they strive to protect their client and complete their mission."
