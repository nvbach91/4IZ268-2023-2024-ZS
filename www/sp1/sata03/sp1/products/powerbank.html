Stolp is a Faraday cage for smartphones that keeps out incoming and outgoing radio signals. Cellular, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth - any wireless connection is blocked. What for? To give you privacy, concentration and peace of mind;

Stolp Recharge includes a wireless power bank to charge your phone while you recharge with a full life outside the digital world.

Features:

  • Detachable wireless powerbank
  • Compatible with all phones that support Qi wireless charging
  • Capacity: 10,000 mAh. Charging capacity: 5W
  • Powerbank capacity enough for three full charges of the battery (depending on the phone model)
  • 100% offline - blocks incoming and outgoing radio signals
