NEARBY ACTIVITIES

Sky Walk

Ever wanted to touch the skies? If yes, then this is your best shot. 1116 meters above mean sea level dominates its surrounding mountains.

You can get up by taking modern and fast ski lift, or by hiking up those steep slopes.

Mammoth Water Park

Ideal place for everyone, who ever loved playing with water.

A paradise of water fun with an unexplored island and a playhouse right in the middle of a giant body of water.

The playful 52-meter river will offer splashing and water fun for the little ones, even for those who prefer ankle-deep water.

Great Roler Coaster

Get ready for an adrenaline ride of 3 kilometers through forest terrain and open plains.

At an elevation of 364 meters, you will drive through 25 lightning-fast turns, a double 360° carousel, a figure eight loop, an underground tunnel and swing on 4 great jumps over forest paths.
