Forest Edge Cabin

Although oldest of our cabins, it is definitely the most charismatic one.

Just few steps from the forest, it is also the furthest one from the center. This adds desired privacy and on top of that, in the winter months, as this is the highest placed of our cabins, you can fairly easily travel on skies right to the nearest slope.

Cliff Side Cabin

The one placed at a small hill, looking like hanging from the cliff observing the valley.

The one with the best view, as many tend to call it, this cabin offers you breathtaking view, that gives you the whole Valley on the palm of your hand, with near mountains topping the background.

White Rock Cabin

The comfiest one and ideal for outside parties.

This cabin is the closest to the centre, which means you have a commons close. Do not worry about the noise, they are separated enough that if any party was happening, your sleep would not be disturbed.

On top of that it also has its own small fireplace with few chairs for you to enjoy a night outside.

Cedar Brook Cabin

Youngest cabin of them all, but also the most modern one.
