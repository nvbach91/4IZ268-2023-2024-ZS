Our Story

As a life long admirers of Jeseniky region, and especially of a small city Dolni Morava, it was of no question when it came up for a question where would we want to live.

And since we think this region has an amazing potencial, we decided to build luxurious place for people to come and stay, either for relax in nature, or more adventurous approach.

Our cabins provide you with experiences you will take away as memories. Whether you are a busy professional looking for an escape from city life, a family looking for a meaningful time away with your kids or a large group wanting to rent multiple units with a family reunion in mind, we believe our cabins are the perfect place for you, providing comfort, relaxation and an inspiring stay.
