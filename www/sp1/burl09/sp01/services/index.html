LB Services

Do you have a piano at home that needs tuning? Are you moving house and need to move your piano? Or does your damaged piano need repair or refurbishment? Then our services are just for you. We tune, repair, refurbish and in short take care of pianos all over Czech Republic and the neighbouring countries. Expect a professional approach backed by many years of experience and quality references.

Tuning

Under no circumstances try to tune the piano yourself! You can make the instrument even more out of tune, or even damage it. It is no coincidence that piano tuning has historically been a highly respected profession. Tuning a piano (or more correctly, an upright or grand piano) is an art that requires many hours of practice, a great ear for music and a keen sense of touch. Entrust your piano to us and it will be in the best hands. We have experience working for important and demanding clients.

We provide:

  • tuning and servicing pianos in customers' homes,
  • concert and studio service and tuning,
  • regulation and repair of the action mechanism, on site or in the workshop,
  • intonation of the keys, refinement of the sound and tone colour.

Moving

There are places where moving the piano is not feasible. For example, narrow hallways in the building, narrow spiral staircases, and in some cases you simply do not want us to move down your new staircase. In these cases, we are able to offer you a piano removal through a window. Know that moving your piano will be no problem with our company!

Repairs and renovation

We carry out all servicing work and complete refurbishment of old pianos and upright pianos. We can set in motion mechanics that have not been used for years, replace strings, tuning pins and soundboards. We can handle all shop repairs, sealcoating and refinishing with the original age-tested shellac polish technology.
