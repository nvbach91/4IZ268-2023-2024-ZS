The Belgian Blue are joining us for a show!

Woo-hoo!

After last year's show at Kampus Hybernská with The Ocelots, we're excited to announce that this year, we're continuing the tradition with our friends from The Belgian Blue. The're coming to Prague in December for a show with us, so don't forget to stop by at Kampus Hybernská on December 7th, 2023, for a night full of good music and good times.

For those who haven't met The Belgian Blue yet, they're an indie-folk project from Vienna, Austria drawing from the traditional folk music of Appalachia and Ireland. In line with folk tradition, their songs paint a vibrant portrayal of everyday people and experiences and after first seeing them live earlier this year, we're excited to see what they have in store this time!
