Meet Jiný Metro

About the band

Jiný Metro is a Prague-based alternative folk duo consisting of Klára Čmejrková and Katie Alžběta Brown. The duo met in grammar school, where they began singing together and have been friends ever since. Since the release of their first album in 2021, their repertoire has expanded by hours worth of catchy and witty songs about everyday life, relationships and hypothetical situations. Sung in Czech, English and French, their duets are accompanied by electric guitar, bass guitar, cello, ukulele, flute, clarinet, keyboards, percussion, and toy saxophone. Since 2022, the band has been performing with multi-instrumentalist Alex Matějíček.

2018-2019

The early years of Jiný Metro. Klára and Katie were classmates at school and wrote their first song, Vysavač, to serve as background music for an assignment. The girls continued to create music together, performing around Prague and writing most of the songs that would later appear on the Rande s revizorem album. In respect to their genre of music, the duo chose to use the Czech name Jiný Metro, meaning "alternative underground".

2020-2021

During a small break between waves of COVID, the girls began to compile and record their songs on an album. For one of the songs, titled French Song, they created their first and only music video, which was released on YouTube in December 2020. The full album was released on Spotify on June 4th, 2021, and was titled Rande s revizorem, meaning "a date with the ticket inspector". Both members of Jiný Metro decided to deepen their knowledge of music, with Klára enrolling in the music composition program at the Prague Conservatory and Katie enrolling in the flute program at the Jan Deyl Conservatory.

2022-2023

In summer 2022, the band was joined by Alex Matějíček, a fellow composition student at the Prague Conservatory. As a trio, they performed their 100th show, and they continue to work on new music. In 2023, the band began to play its first international shows in Leipzig, Germany and Eibenthal, Romania, and is set to release an EP in early 2024.
