Introduction

Welcome to the realm of gaming greatness, where Valve Corporation reigns supreme! Founded in 1996 by the legendary Gabe Newell, also known as the meme lord and sultan of Steam, Valve has left an indelible mark on the gaming cosmos. Gabe, with his iconic beard and laid-back vibe, co-created gaming gems like Half-Life and Portal, unleashing them onto a world hungry for innovation. Valve's playground also includes the ever-dominant Steam platform, where gamers unite to swap stories, frags, and, of course, memes. So buckle up, because in Valve's world, the game is not just a game; it's a journey, a culture, and an ever-expanding universe of epic gaming moments.
