@Maf1aBo$$ playing Mafia: Definitive Edition

I was skeptical of buying this remake when comparing it to Mafia 2 Definitive, but I read IGN's review and it looked pretty good. I'm impressed. Ryzen 5 3600, GTX 2060 6gb. 16gb Ram. 50-60 FPS. High settings. A few times it has clipped but I think it's the checkpoint saving. Driving simulation is great, controls are good, after re-mapping some functions the game has been very enjoyable so far. Free ride opened up after I checked the menu after mission 4. Could have been earlier but I couldn't stop playing. Very well worth 39.99. I've paid more for worse games in the past six months.
